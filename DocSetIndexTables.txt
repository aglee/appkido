Tables we use in the dsidx SQLite database. ZTOKEN is the main table, and ZTOKENMETAINFORMATION gives us additional information about tokens.

ZTOKEN
    ZTOKENNAME
    ZLANGUAGE => ZAPILANGUAGE
    ZTOKENTYPE => ZTOKENTYPE
    ZMETAINFORMATION => ZTOKENMETAINFORMATION

ZTOKENMETAINFORMATION
    ZTOKEN => ZTOKEN
    ZFILE => ZFILEPATH
    ZDECLAREDIN => ZHEADER
    ZRETURNVALUE => ZRETURNVALUE
    ZANCHOR
    ZDECLARATION
    ZDEPRECATIONSUMMARY
    ZABSTRACT

These tables are mostly lists of strings, although ZHEADER also has a ZFRAMEWORKNAME column

ZAPILANGUAGE
    ZFULLNAME

ZFILEPATH
    ZPATH

ZHEADER
    ZHEADERPATH
    ZFRAMEWORKNAME

ZRETURNVALUE
    ZABSTRACT

ZTOKENTYPE
